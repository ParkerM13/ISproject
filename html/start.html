League of Legends is a online game. It fits within the MOBA (massive online battle arena) genre. The game is played between two teams of five players. Each team will have a Top lane, Jungle, Mid Lane, ADC (Attack Damage Carry), and Support. Players chose from the 160+ champion choices to play their role, work with their team, and take the other team's nexus. The map is square, with three lanes (top, middle, bottom) and the jungle between them. Top lane champions are typically the tank for the team. Their position is also referred to as an island because they're left out of the action until later in the game. Mid lane champions are the mages and assassins. Their position in the middle of the map allows for them to have big impact as magic damage, or back line killers. The ADC and Support share the bottom lane. ADCs are squishy champions that auto attack. They start off weak, but after leveling up and getting items they become a force to fear. Support champions are there to assist the ADCs.
